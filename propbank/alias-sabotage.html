sabotage.01 - wreck clandestinely

SABOTAGE-V NOTES: No Vncls. Beautiful etymology behind this. During the Industrial Revolution inFrance, disgruntled (and displaced) workers would toss their woodenshoes, called "sabot"s, into the machinery of the new factories,naturally causing the machinery to break. This kind of willfuldestruction came to be called sabot-age. (from sabotage.01-v)
